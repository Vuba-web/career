About CareerTicket & Skills Packages

Career Ticket by Umurava is designed to equip young individuals with the skills they need to thrive in the 21st-century workforce. This comprehensive program covers key areas of Digital Skills, Soft Skills, Workplace Tools, and Career Path Development, ensuring that participants are ready for freelancing, remote work, consulting, entrepreneurship, and more.
